Headings

Headings are the elements of HTML. They are used to categorise the content in a hieracchy, or based on importance level.

Levels of Headings

There are six levels of headings as follows.

Most Important : Heading Level 1

More Important : Heading Level 2

Important : Heading Level 3

Less Important : Heading Level 4

Lesser Important : Heading Level 5
Least Important : Heading Level 6
